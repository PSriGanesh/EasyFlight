HYDERABAD

Hyderabad, the city of Nizams is a perfect blend of vibrant culture with glorious history. The touch of its color past is still visible not just in the monumental buildings but also in the life of its residents and their cultural practices. The fifth largest city of the country and the capital city of Telangana, Hyderabad offers amazing holidaying choices to everyone.

The top places to visit in Hyderabad would include the Charminar, Golconda Fort, Salar Jung Museum, Chowmahalla Palace, Hussain Sagar, Birla Mandir, Ramoji Film City, Nehru Zoological Park, Qutb Shahi Tombs, Mecca Masjid and many more.
