Frequently Asked Questions

1. What if I exceed my free baggage weight allowance?

Customers carrying more than the allowed baggage limit will be charged excess baggage fees at the time of check-in

2. What items are not permitted on my Flight?

  1. Compressed gases - deeply refrigerated, flammable, non-flammable and poisonous such as butane oxygen, liquid nitrogen, aqualung cylinders and compressed gas cylinders
  2. Corrosives such as acids, alkalis, mercury and wet cell batteries and apparatus containing mercury
  3. Explosives, munitions, fireworks and flares, ammunition including blank cartridges, handguns, fire works, pistol caps
  4. Flammable liquids and solids such as lighter refills, lighter fuel, matches, paints, thinners, fire-lighters, lighters that need inverting before ignition, matches (these may be carried on the person), radioactive material, briefcases and attache case with installed alarm devices
  5. Oxidizing materials such as bleaching powder and peroxides
  6. Poisons and infectious substances such as insecticides, weed-killers and live virus materials

3. Are there any general advisories that I should keep in mind regarding my baggage?

Don't leave baggage unobserved at any time, especially at the airport. Unattended baggage may be removed by Airport Security as an object of suspicion

4. How can I change/ cancel my reservation?

You can postpone your flight at no extra cost, but cancelling your flight will be charged as cancellation fees.

5. Would I be able to cancel my ticket and use the same ticket in the future?

No, you cannot. Once the ticket is cancelled, you cannot use it anywhere else.
